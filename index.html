Woods & Burger Adventure


Adam and I had planned a trip hiking in the White Mountains. What we did not anticipate was how much rain there would be.


After a long day with many miles hiked and soaked clothing and gear we set up for the night. It was then we discovered a hole in the tent. This made our sleeping far less comfortable and dry than we hoped.


The next day featured many more miles of hiking, none of which were more dry than the day before. So we used some of those miles to leave the wilderness.

  2. Buy hamburgers
  3. Eat hamburgers
Burgers are delicious, especially when you're exhausted.
